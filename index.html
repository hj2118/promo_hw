Cha, Il-Man (차일만)

(1952-)

Personal Inforamation

Born in 1954 in South Korea

Awards

  • 1987 - 1st International Artist Award (People To People)
  • 1988 - U.S. National Assembly Culture Award

Realistic expressionism

  • Expresses secrets hidden in nature and in light, which is the source of life
  • Depicts the wide sea and sky based on the scenery of the travel destinations (e.g. Paris, Spain)

  • Felt wonder and beauty in the phenomenon that the light temporarily becomes brighter just before sunset
  • Lives and nature often exude the most profound and beautiful appearance at the end
